Sleep: 
●	Lasts for 3 rounds
●	Doesn’t end on switching out
●	50% chance of waking up on being damaged
●	You can’t move or use a non continuous ability.
Poison:
●	Lasts for 3 rounds
●	Doesn’t end on switching out.
●	You lose 5%, 10%, 15% hp in 3 rounds respectively
●	Steel and poison types can’t be poisoned
Burn: 
●	Lasts for 3 rounds
●	Doesn’t end on switching out.
●	You lose 10% hp every round.
●	Your Atk stat is lowered by 20%.
●	Fire types cannot be burnt.
Frozen:
●	Lasts for 3 rounds.
●	Doesn’t end on switching out.
●	You can’t move.
●	Frozen ends on being hit with a fire, steel or fighting move.
●	Ice types can’t be frozen.
Paralysis:
●	Lasts for 3 rounds.
●	Doesn’t end on switching out.
●	Your speed is halved.
●	You have a 50% chance to move.
●	Ground & electric types can’t be paralyzed.
Confusion:
●	Lasts for 3 rounds.
●	Doesn’t end on switching out.
●	50% chance to move or deal 50 damage to self.
●	If you are able to move, then there's a 25% chance that the confusion will end.
Infatuation:
●	Lasts for 3 rounds.
●	Doesn't end on switching out.
●	You can't hit the foe that infatuated you.
●	If you are damaged, 50% chance the infatuation will end.
Bad Poison:
●	Lasts forever.
●	Starting from 10%, the damage of bad Poison will increase every round by 2%
●	Other points are the same as poison.
Severe Burn:
●	Lasts forever.
●	Your Atk stat is lowered by 50%.
●	Other points are the same as burn.
Neuro Paralysis:
●	Lasts forever.
●	You can't use priority moves.
●	Other points are the same as paralysis.
Curse:
●	You lose 30% hp at the end of every round.
●	Lasts forever.
●	Doesn't end on switching out.
Deep sleep:
●	Lasts forever.
●	30% chance of waking up on being damaged.
●	Other points are the same as sleep.
